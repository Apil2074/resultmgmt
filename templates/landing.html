Powerful Features
Built for Modern Schools

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Manage multiple schools from a single super-admin dashboard. Each school gets its own isolated environment, admins, and custom settings.

Automated Grading

Input raw marks and let the system automatically calculate grades, GPAs, and aggregate scores based on your school's unique grading rules.

Dynamic Reports & Ledgers

Generate comprehensive mark ledgers and individual student grade sheets instantly. Export to print-ready formats with a single click.

Public Student Portal

Students and parents can securely check their academic results online using their Date of Birth and Roll Number without requiring accounts.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions for Super Admins, School Admins, and Teachers. Ensure staff only have access to the classes and subjects they manage.

Subject & Exam Management

Easily configure subjects (theory/practical splits), link them to specific classes, and schedule term-wise examinations effortlessly.
