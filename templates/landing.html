Performance Analytics Dashboard

Visualize class-wide performance trends with interactive bar charts and line graphs. Instantly spot top performers, struggling students, and subject-level patterns.

Dynamic Reports & Ledgers

Generate comprehensive mark ledgers and individual student grade sheets instantly. Export to print-ready formats with a single click, complete with visual grade breakdowns.

Grade Distribution Heatmaps

Identify knowledge gaps across subjects with color-coded heatmaps. Understand exactly which topics need more attention at a class or student level.

Public Student Portal

Students and parents can securely check their academic results online using their Date of Birth and Roll Number — no account required.

Automated Grading & GPA

Input raw marks and the system automatically computes grades, GPA, CGPA, and aggregate scores based on your school's unique grading boundaries.

Subject & Exam Management

Easily configure subjects (theory/practical splits), link them to specific classes, and schedule term-wise examinations — then track results with rich analytics.
